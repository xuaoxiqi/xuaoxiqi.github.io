About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is an Associate Professor in fluid mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). His research focuses on turbulent flows and coupled heat and mass transport. He earned his bachelor's degree in theoretical and applied mechanics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013 and his Ph.D. in mechanical engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. He has published 44 peer-reviewed journal articles, including 24 as first or corresponding author. These papers have received over 2,000 citations, with an h-index of 25 (Scopus). He also serves as an Associate Editor of the International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (Elsevier).
