About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is currently Research Associate of the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). He received his bachelor's degree in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013, and PhD degree in Mechanical Engineering from HKUST in 2017. Dr. Xu's research is focused on numerical modeling and simulation of complex transport phenomena at mesoscopic and microscopic levels. Specifically, fluid flows and mass transfer in porous media, particulate flows, and gas-liquid two-phase flows at mesoscopic level; adsorbates on surfaces, intrinsic transport properties of active species at microscopic level. He has published 16 papers in various prestigious journals, among which 7 papers as first author. These papers have collectively received more than 220 citations and earned Dr. Xu an h-index of 10 (Google Scholar). In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as the Managing Editor of Applied Thermal Engineering (Elsevier) and an active reviewer for over 10 academic journals.
