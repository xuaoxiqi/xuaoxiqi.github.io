About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is an associate professor of fluid mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in theoretical and applied mechanics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013, and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. Dr. Xu's research is focused on turbulence structure and transport mechanism. He has published 36 papers in various prestigious journals, among which 18 papers as the first author or corresponding author. These papers have collectively received more than 1300 citations and earned Dr. Xu an h-index of 21 (Google Scholar). In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as associate editor of International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (2021 - ), and he served as managing editor of Applied Thermal Engineering (2017-2018).
