About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is currently Associate Professor of the Department of Fluid Mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013, and PhD degree in Mechanical Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. He then worked as Research Associate at HKUST for one year and joined NPU in 2018. Dr. Xu's research is focused on numerical modeling and simulation of complex transport phenomena with emphasis on fluid flows and heat transfer. He has published 16 papers in various prestigious journals, among which 7 papers as first author. These papers have collectively received more than 250 citations and earned Dr. Xu an h-index of 10 (Google Scholar). In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as the Managing Editor of Applied Thermal Engineering (Elsevier) and an active reviewer for over 10 academic journals.
