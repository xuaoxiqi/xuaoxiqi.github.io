Dr. Ao Xu is an Associate Professor of fluid mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in theoretical and applied mechanics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013 and his Ph.D. in mechanical engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. Dr. Xu's current research focuses on turbulent flows coupled with heat and mass transport. He has published 44 papers in prestigious journals, including 24 as first author or corresponding author. He serves as Associate Editor of the International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (Elsevier). These publications have collectively received over 2,000 citations, earning him an h-index of 25 (Scopus).
