About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is Associate Professor of Fluid Mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013, and PhD degree in Mechanical Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. Dr. Xu's research is focused on heat transfer in complex fluids. He has published 22 papers in various prestigious journals, among which 10 papers as first author. These papers have collectively received more than 460 citations and earned Dr. Xu an h-index of 12 (Google Scholar). He is principle investigator of NSFC Young Scientists Fund and co-investigator of RGC General Research Fund. In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as Managing Editor of International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (Elsevier) and served as Managing Editor of Applied Thermal Engineering (Elsevier).
