About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is an associate professor of fluid mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in theoretical and applied mechanics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013, and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. Dr. Xu's research is focused on heat transfer in complex fluids. He has published 28 papers in various prestigious journals, among which 13 papers as the first author or corresponding author. These papers have collectively received more than 700 citations and earned Dr. Xu an h-index of 15 (Google Scholar). He is the principal investigator of the NSFC Young Scientists Fund, National Key Laboratory Fund, National Key Project, and co-investigator of the RGC General Research Fund. In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as managing editor of International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (Elsevier), and he served as managing editor of Applied Thermal Engineering (Elsevier).
