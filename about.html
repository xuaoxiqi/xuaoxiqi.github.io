About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is an associate professor of fluid mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in theoretical and applied mechanics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013 and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. Dr. Xu's current research is focused on turbulent transport and particle migration. He has published 42 papers in various prestigious journals, among which 22 papers are as the first author or corresponding author. These papers have collectively received more than 1700 citations, earning Dr. Xu an h-index of 24 (Scopus). In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as an Associate Editor of International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer.
