About Ao

Dr. Ao Xu is an Associate Professor of fluid mechanics at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). He received his bachelor's degree in theoretical and applied mechanics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013 and his Ph.D. in mechanical engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2017. Dr. Xu's current research focuses on turbulent flows coupled with heat and mass transport. He has published 43 papers in prestigious journals, including 23 as first author or corresponding author. These publications have collectively received over 1,900 citations, earning him an h-index of 25 (Scopus). In the international community, Dr. Xu serves as an Associate Editor for International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (Elsevier).
